Step by Step Maggi Recipe Instruction:

  • Take a pan with water.
  • Put pan on the burner.
  • Switch on the burner
  • Boil the water for 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Put maggi and masala in pan.
  • Give 2 minutes, Maggi will be ready.
  • Turn off the burner and start Eating.
