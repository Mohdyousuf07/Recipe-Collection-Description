Step by Step Pasta Recipe Instruction:

  1. Boil Water:

    2. Start with a very large pot of water, about 6 quarts per pound of pasta, Bring it to rapid boil

  2. Add Salt:

    3. Drop in the pasta while the water is at a boil and give it a gentle stir.

  3. Add the Pasta:

    4. Drop in the pasta while the water is at a boil and give it a gentle stir.

  4. Stir:

    5. Stir it occasionally while it cooks. Use the cooking time on the pasta package as a guide.

  5. Taste the Pasta:

    6. Sample the pasta at about 2 minutes shy of the indicated time. It should be al dente. You'll finish cooking it in the sauce.

  6. Drain:

    7. Drain the pasta. If you have a pot with a colander insert, use it to drain the pasta.

  7. Removing Ravioli:

    8. If you're cooking ravioli,use a slotted skimmer and gently lift the ravioli a few at a time.

  8. Stir In the Sauce:

    9. Add the pasta to your sauce and finish cooking it in the sauce.Give it a stir to coat

  9. Add Pasta Water:

    10. Pasta water helps to thicken the sauce.

  10. Buon Appetio:

    11. Follow your recipe's instructions for finishing the dish. And that's all there is to it.Pertecto!
